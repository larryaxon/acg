City Hosted is a business-class hosted phone service that is easy to install, easy to use, and easy to manage. It's provisioned across our redundant and diverse Class 5 Metaswitch backbone with 99.95% uptime. The simple web interface allows you or your authorized dealer to control call forwarding, voicemail handling, and much more.

To get started with City Hosted, you need:
1. A Qualified IP Phone
2. A Quality Internet connection (test your connection)
We will be happy to evalute your current network and switches to make sure that you are ready for hosted. Contact us or one of our authorized dealers to get started today.
